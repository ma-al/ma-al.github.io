    Recent

    June 2016
    Participated in Random Hacks of Kindness (RHoK), a hackathon for good causes. Helped Changing Places develop an online feedback tool for severely disabled people. Follow tweets of the event from @rhokmelb and @acd_eddie, and read the short write-up by Stefan. Shout outs to Eddie, Daniel, Stefan, Ramji and Zinzi.

    May 2016
    Presented at ANZDMC 2016, a disaster and emergency management conference. My talk was entitled "Mapping Wildfire Community Impact" and focused on the optimal placement of sensors in remote areas to detect wildfires. See tweet of event. Based on a paper (currently under review) co-authored with David, Peter and Kent.

    Jan 2016
    Journal article published in Simulation Modelling Practice and Theory (SimPAT) entitled "Simulation of Wildfire Evacuation with Dynamic Factors and Model Composition". Based on work done for IBM Evacuation Planner. Co-authors were Anton, Ermyas, Jan and Kent.
